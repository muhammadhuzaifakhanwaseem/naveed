Child Safety Standards Policy

At OpecFuture, we are committed to providing a safe, educational, and enjoyable experience for all users, including children. We understand the responsibility of ensuring that digital environments are safe for young users, and we adhere to strict guidelines to meet these standards.

Our Commitment to Child Safety:

  • No Harmful Content: OpecFuture does not contain harmful, inappropriate, or age-restricted content. We ensure that all materials and interactions within the app are safe, child-friendly, and suitable for all ages.
  • No Data Collection from Children: We do not collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. In compliance with data protection regulations, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we do not collect or share any data from users in this age group without parental consent.
  • No In-App Purchases or Ads Targeted to Children: OpecFuture does not feature in-app purchases or advertisements designed for children. This means there are no hidden charges or exposure to potentially inappropriate ads for young users.
  • Age Appropriateness: All features and content within OpecFuture are carefully designed to be appropriate for children. We ensure that the app provides a positive, engaging, and educational experience that is safe for kids to use.
  • Parental Controls: OpecFuture includes settings and features that allow parents to monitor and manage their child’s use of the app. We provide clear instructions on how parents can ensure a safe and controlled experience for their children.
  • Safe Environment: We continuously review and update OpecFuture to adhere to the highest child safety standards, ensuring a secure environment for all users.

By using OpecFuture, parents can feel confident that their children are engaging with a safe and positive digital space.
